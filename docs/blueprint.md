# **App Name**: Sakura Style

## Core Features:

- Gender Preference Dialog: Display a gender preference dialog on first page load to tailor the browsing experience with options: Men, Women, Unisex, Skip/Show everything, and a 'Remember my preference' checkbox. The modal is dismissable, non-blocking, accessible, and saves a simple flag in localStorage if the user opts in.
- Home Page: Home page featuring a hero section, USP, featured Men, Women, and Unisex collections, a seasonal drop section, an ethical sourcing blurb, and a newsletter opt-in form.
- Collection Pages: Collection pages for Men, Women, Unisex, Traditional, Streetwear and Minimal Workwear, each with 6-12 product cards (mock products ok) and filter/sort functionality.
- About Page: About page with a brand story, details on materials and sustainability practices, and information about small maker partnerships in Japan.
- Journal Page: Journal page featuring three original articles (400â€“600 words each) on Japanese fashion culture, craftsmanship, and styling guides.
- Contact Form: Contact page with a simple form (name, email, message) for user inquiries.
- Policies Page: Policies page covering Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Shipping & Returns, and an Ads & Cookies page explaining ad/analytics tags, consent, and opt-out options.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Cherry blossom pink (#F7B7CD), evoking softness and springtime in Japan.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F5F5F5), providing a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#70A1AF) complements the pink and gray, suggesting tradition with a touch of modernity.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for readability and a touch of warmth.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for elegance, balanced by a modern feel. This will pair well with PT Sans, while lending the headlines more visual presence.
- Minimalist icons with a clean, modern aesthetic.
- Mobile-first design with a clean, uncluttered layout that emphasizes whitespace and showcases the clothing.
